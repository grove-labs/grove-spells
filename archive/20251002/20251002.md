# 2025-10-02 Grove Spell

## Forum Post

[Forum Post Link](https://forum.sky.money/t/october-2-2025-proposed-changes-to-grove-for-upcoming-spell/27190)

## List of intended changes

### Mainnet
- Onboard JTRSY crosschain transfers to Plume
  - Centrifuge Id for Plume 4 (`PLUME_DESTINATION_CENTRIFUGE_ID`)
  - transfer max 20M (`JTRSY_CROSSCHAIN_TRANSFER_RATE_LIMIT_MAX`)
  - transfer slope 20M (`JTRSY_CROSSCHAIN_TRANSFER_RATE_LIMIT_SLOPE`)

### Plume
- Initialize Grove Liquidity Layer on Plume
- Onboard Centrifuge ACRDX
  - deposit max 20M (`PLUME_ACRDX_DEPOSIT_RATE_LIMIT_MAX`)
  - deposit slope 20M (`PLUME_ACRDX_DEPOSIT_RATE_LIMIT_SLOPE`)
  - redeem max unlimited
  - redeem slope 0
- Onboard Centrifuge JTRSY (redemptions only)
  - redeem max 20M (`PLUME_JTRSY_REDEEM_RATE_LIMIT_MAX`)
  - redeem slope 20M (`PLUME_JTRSY_REDEEM_RATE_LIMIT_SLOPE`)
