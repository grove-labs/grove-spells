## List of intended changes

### Mainnet
- Enable CCTP transfers to Avalache instance of Grove Liquidity Layer
    - set unlimited global limit for joint CCTP transfers
    - set max 50M USDC rate limit transfer with slope of 50M USDC per day for CCTP transfers to Avalanche
- Onboard Ethena
    - Minting rate limited at 250M USDC max and 100M USDC per day slope
    - Burning rate limited at 500M USDe max and 200M USDe per day slope
    - Depositing into sUSDe rate limited at 250M USDe max and 100M USDe per day slope
    - Cooldown not rate limited (set to unlimited)
### Avalanche
- Initialize Grove Liquidity Layer instance on Avalanche
- Enable CCTP transfers to Ethereum Mainnet instance of Grove Liquidity Layer
    - set unlimited global limit for joint CCTP transfers
    - set max 50M USDC rate limit transfer with slope of 50M USDC per day for CCTP transfers to Ethereum Mainnet
