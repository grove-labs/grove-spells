## List of intended changes

### Mainnet
- Onboard Securitize Tokenized AAA CLO Fund
  - deposit max 50M (`SECURITIZE_STAC_USDC_DEPOSIT_MAX`)
  - deposit slope 50M (`SECURITIZE_STAC_USDC_DEPOSIT_SLOPE`)
  - redeem max unlimited
  - redeem slope 0
- Onboard Galaxy Arch CLO Fund
  - deposit max 50M (`GALAXY_ARCH_CLOS_USDC_DEPOSIT_MAX`)
  - deposit slope 50M (`GALAXY_ARCH_CLOS_USDC_DEPOSIT_SLOPE`)
- Onboard Morpho Grove x Steakhouse USDC High Yield Vault
  - deposit max 20M (`GROVE_X_STEAKHOUSE_USDC_MORPHO_VAULT_DEPOSIT_MAX`)
  - deposit slope 20M (`GROVE_X_STEAKHOUSE_USDC_MORPHO_VAULT_DEPOSIT_SLOPE`)
  - redeem max unlimited
  - redeem slope 0
- Onboard Agora AUSD
  - deposit max 50M (`AGORA_AUSD_USDC_MINT_MAX`)
  - deposit slope 50M (`AGORA_AUSD_USDC_MINT_SLOPE`)
  - redeem max unlimited
  - redeem slope 0
- Onboard Ripple RLUSD
  - deposit max 50M (`RIPPLE_RLUSD_USDC_MINT_MAX `)
  - deposit slope 50M (`RIPPLE_RLUSD_USDC_MINT_SLOPE `)
  - redeem max 50M (`RIPPLE_RLUSD_USDC_BURN_MAX `)
  - redeem slope 50M (`RIPPLE_RLUSD_USDC_BURN_SLOPE`)
